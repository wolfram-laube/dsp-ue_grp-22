\item[(e)]
This function calculates the total energy of a signal:

\begin{verbatim}
def energy(x):
    """Calculates the total energy of the signal."""
    return np.sum(np.abs(x)**2)
\end{verbatim}

and yields:
\begin{verbatim}
Energy x_1: 37
Energy x_2: 48.03937311125317
Energy x_3: 1152.0
Energy x_4: 128.99999999999994
\end{verbatim}
